
1. BINARY SEARCH


Time Complexity:
- Best Case: O(1)
- Average Case: O(log n)
- Worst Case: O(log n)

Space Complexity:
- O(1) (iterative approach, no extra space)



2A. SUBSET SUM VERIFICATION


Time Complexity:
- O(n) (traverses all elements once)

Space Complexity:
- O(1) (uses only a single variable for sum)



2B. SUBSET SUM DECISION (RECURSIVE)


Time Complexity:
- O(2^n) (each element has two choices: include or exclude)

Space Complexity:
- O(n) (recursion stack depth)



3. TRAVELLING SALESMAN PROBLEM (BRUTE FORCE)


Time Complexity:
- O(n!) (all permutations of cities are generated)

Space Complexity:
- O(n) (stores permutation and recursion/iteration overhead)
